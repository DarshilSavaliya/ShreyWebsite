About Doors County

Founded in 2005 in St. Louis, MO, Doors County has been a family-owned leader in energy-efficient windows and custom doors. Our mission is to combine modern design with durable quality, ensuring every installation stands the test of time.

Our Values

Our Journey

  • 2005: Doors County founded in St. Louis, MO.
  • 2010: Expanded services to include commercial installations.
  • 2015: Launched energy-efficient window line.
  • 2020: Celebrated 15 years with 5,000+ satisfied customers.
  • 2023: Opened new training center for installers.
  • Quality Craftsmanship – We never compromise on materials or installation standards.
  • Eco-Friendly Solutions – All our windows meet ENERGY STAR® guidelines.
  • Customer First – Transparent quotes, prompt service, and satisfaction guarantees.

Our Mission

To provide top-quality doors and windows with unparalleled customer service, enhancing comfort and efficiency for every home.

Our Vision

To be the leading door & window provider in Missouri, known for innovation, integrity, and excellence.
